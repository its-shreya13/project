Exploring the Universe with Stellarix

Welcome to the Stellarix, your go-to platform for real-time information on celestial events, satellite positions, and upcoming space launches. This dashboard is designed for space enthusiasts, students, and anyone curious about the wonders of our universe.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make space exploration more accessible and engaging. We believe that by providing real-time data in an easy-to-understand format, we can inspire a new generation of space enthusiasts and foster a deeper appreciation for the cosmos.

Features

Explore a range of features, including:

  • Live tracking of satellite positions
  • Upcoming space launch schedules
  • Astronomical picture of the day

Meet the Team

Our passionate team of developers and space enthusiasts is dedicated to bringing you the latest and most accurate information. We combine our love for space with our expertise in web development to create an immersive experience for users.
