How Much Do You Know About Satellites?


Quiz

Earth is orbited by a diverse array of satellites, numbering in the thousands. These satellites serve various purposes, enhancing communication, weather forecasting, scientific research, and national security. Communication satellites facilitate global connectivity, while Earth observation satellites capture essential data for scientific, environmental, and commercial applications. Navigation satellites, like those in the GPS system, provide precise positioning information. Scientific satellites contribute to space exploration and research, expanding our understanding of the cosmos. Additionally, military satellites support national security through surveillance and communication capabilities. As technology advances, private companies and international collaborations continue to launch new satellites, contributing to the dynamic network in Earth's orbit.
